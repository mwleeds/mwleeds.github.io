Camping


On-site camping is free. You’ll be in a beautiful meadow under walnut and apple trees with access to restrooms and showers. If it's your style, we highly recommend camping to get the most out of the incredible venue!


Hotels

If you’d rather stay in a hotel, we recommend booking early - the holiday weekend will be busy in Southern Oregon. There are many options in Jacksonville, Medford, and Ashland. We have some discounted rooms reserved already at Wine Country Inn Jacksonville; contact us if you'd like one of those. Here are a few other options:
